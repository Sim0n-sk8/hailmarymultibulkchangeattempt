Myopia, or short-sightedness, is on the rise worldwide. Children today are developing myopia at younger ages than ever before, and cases are progressing faster. Left unchecked, myopia can increase the risk of serious long-term eye health issues like retinal detachment, glaucoma, and macular degeneration.

While glasses and contact lenses have traditionally helped correct blurry distance vision, they don’t address the root issue—managing the progression of myopia. Fortunately, new research and technology now make it possible to slow down myopia in growing children.

What Is MiYOSMART?

MiYOSMART lenses are a new generation of spectacle lenses designed specifically for myopia control. Developed by Hoya in partnership with The Hong Kong Polytechnic University, MiYOSMART is backed by over 5 years of global clinical research and real-world success.

Unlike standard single-vision lenses that only sharpen distance vision, MiYOSMART lenses use a unique technology called D.I.M.S. (Defocus Incorporated Multiple Segments). This provides clear vision while actively reducing the stimulus that causes the eye to grow longer, a key factor in myopia progression.

Why Parents Are Choosing MiYOSMART

  • Non-invasive and safe: These lenses look and feel just like regular glasses—no drops, surgery, or contact lenses required.
  • Clinically proven: Studies show MiYOSMART can slow myopia progression by an average of 60% in children.
  • Designed for active kids: Made with impact-resistant material and UV protection, MiYOSMART is perfect for young wearers.

Where to Start

Only certified optometrists and practices can offer MiYOSMART lenses. These professionals have received training in myopia management and will guide you through a personalised care plan to track progress and support your child’s long-term eye health.
